KUND MALIR BEACH

Kund Malir' is a beach in Balochistan, Pakistan located in Hingol National Park, about 150 kilometers from Zero-Point on Makran Coastal Highway.[1] It is located 236.8 kilometers west of Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan.[2] The drive between Kund Malir and Ormara is considered to be scenic.

PRINCESS OF HOPE

The Princess of Hope is a natural rock formation in Pakistan of the type known as a hoodoo or "fairy chimney" and which could fancifully be construed as resembling a crowned and skirted female figure looking toward the horizon. It is situated approximately 190 km from the financial hub of Karachi

ZIARAT

Ziarat is a city in the Ziarat District situated in the Balochistan. It is 130 kilometres from the capital city of Eastern Balochistan Quetta. It was established as a district on 1st July 1986, before then it was part of Sibi district

HINGOL RIVER

Hingol River or Hungol River is located in the Makran region, in the Gwadar District of southeastern Balochistan Province, in southwestern Pakistan. The river and valley are protected within Hingol National Park
